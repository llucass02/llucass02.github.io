Hi This is my final project for CSC 106. is a game based on the original: Flappy Bird. Click on either the 'easy' button or the 'hard' button to select a difficulty. After selecting a difficulty click on the start button to begin. After starting click on the screen to keep Ohnoes off the ground and between the pipes. You get a point every time Ohnoes goes through the pipes without dying. Good Luck and Have fun!
